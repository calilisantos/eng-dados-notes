## **Objetos e privilégios possíveis:**

| **Objeto** | **Privilégios** |
|--------|-------------|
| **Catalog e schema** | `CREATE` E `USAGE` |
| **Table** | `SELECT` E `MODIFY` |
| **View** | `SELECT` |
| **Function** | `EXECUTE` |
| **Storage credential e External location** | `CREATE TABLE`, `READ FILES` E `WRITE FILES` |
| **Share e Recipient** | `SELECT` |
